<h1><strong>School Achievements</strong></h1>
<h3>2025 Green Schools @ South West Award</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/55285040170_d2d6c0b12e_k.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Green Schools @ Southwest Award recognises schools for their efforts
in promoting environmental awareness and sustainability within the school
and wider community. Since the early 2000s, Yuhua Secondary School has
actively participated in the programme through various environmental education
initiatives aimed at cultivating environmentally responsible habits among
students.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that we have attained the Platinum Award for 2025,
sustaining the highest tier of recognition under the programme over the
years as part of its continued commitment towards sustainability!</p>
<h4>2025 School Green Awards (SGA)</h4>
<p>The School Green Awards (SGA) encourages Singapore schools to promote
environmental awareness through customised activities and sustainability
initiatives. Through participation in the programme, students develop good
environmental habits, deepen their understanding of environmental issues
and contribute meaningfully to the wider community.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that our school has attained the Yellow Flame Award
for 2025!</p>
