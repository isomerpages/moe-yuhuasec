<h3>Background of Robotics Club</h3>
<p>Yuhua's Tinkering and Robotics Club encourages students to showcase creativity
and innovation through coding and robotics. Our platform enables students
to apply design thinking to real-world challenges, fostering a healthier
future.</p>
<p>Developing successful robots demands experimentation, imagination, and
dedication. Our student builders and programmers enhance their skills through
hands-on experiences, mastering the full cycle from design to coding whilst
tackling meaningful problems.</p>
<p>The club nurtures members to care, connect, and contribute effectively.
Members develop curiosity, respect, responsibility, and adaptability—becoming
thoughtful innovators and problem-solvers for the future.</p>
