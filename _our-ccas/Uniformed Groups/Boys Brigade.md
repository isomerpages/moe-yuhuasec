<h3>Background</h3>
<p>The Boys’ Brigade (BB) seeks to nurture youth through values-based leadership
and service. Founded on Christian principles, the BB aims to build character,
discipline, and a spirit of care and responsibility in every boy. Guided
by our mission to develop young people with strong moral values and leadership
qualities, we strive to help members grow into confident and compassionate
individuals.</p>
<p>The 28th Singapore Company, co-sponsored by Jurong Christian Church, was
previously based at Shuqun Secondary School and has been part of Yuhua
Secondary School since 2019. Through our programmes, activities, and community
service, we inspire boys to serve others, lead with integrity, and make
a positive difference in their community.</p>
